Cast No Shadow

About the game

In a team of six people, three programmers and three artits we had to make a full game for the course of Game Projects. In a full semester we started from scratch and built an idea for a game and developed it fully. The game is about a vampire family that wakes up in their own home and see that it has been overrun with humans. They need to escape their house and gather their relics while not getting caught.


I worked as a programmer on the project. I made the bat-mechanic, the way you put items on the coffin, the breaking of the vases when they fall on the ground and some of the way the UI works.

Other information

We used Unreal Engine with C++ to make this game.
